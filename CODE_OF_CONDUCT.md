# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Be respectful, constructive, and welcoming. Discuss the work, not the person. Give maintainers and contributors the benefit of the doubt, and make room for people with different backgrounds and levels of experience.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, threats, sustained disruption, and publishing another person's private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, reject, or remove contributions and participation that violate this standard. Serious or repeated violations may result in a temporary or permanent ban from the project.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through their GitHub profile. Reports will be reviewed in good faith and handled as confidentially as practical.
